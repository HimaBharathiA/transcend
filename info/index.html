"So what is Transcend Engineering?



Transcend is a student organization focused on providing invaluable resources and opportunities to students on campus who are interested in creating their own ventures. We do not limit our member’s ideas to tech, apps or purely entrepreneurial ventures; instead we encourage collaboration between majors and focuses which we hope inspires start up ideas that can change the world. Our utility comes from the vast network of alumni and professionals from the Madison area and beyond which we tap regularly for inspiring talks and informative workshops that our members can utilize to expand their knowledge beyond their curriculum and hopefully apply it towards innovation. Our impact goes beyond the resources we provide and the knowledge we impart to our members.


Our core concept is to inspire the Madison student community to create businesses and innovate ideas that will change the world.

Our Transcend Madison Innovation Competition is a great example of how we are aspiring to pave the way at UW for passionate and motivated students to apply their creativity and knowledge for creating amazing things. We hope to make the competition sustainable in the long term and establish a reliable outlet for student creativity. Our competition will highlight the amazing things being done in the Madison community and inspire people to look at the world through the lens of an innovator.
